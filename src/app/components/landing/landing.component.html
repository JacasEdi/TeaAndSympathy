Black Ivy is Edinburgh’s new home from home, for early birds and night owls.

A stylish neighbourhood bar and boutique hotel, we believe in local luxury for all, from breakfast ’til bedtime. Our Drafthouse specialises in crisp tank beer, creative cocktails and warm welcomes; from the kitchen you can expect luxury comfort food with the occasional twist and more-ish bar bites (think Sliders and King Prawn Lollipops) to pair with your pint. Our bedrooms have been uniquely designed to tell a story and, nestled in the heart of leafy Bruntsfield, are the perfect bohemian escape and base to explore.
